Privacy Policy for TS IPTV

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how TS Software Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application TS IPTV (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently, in compliance with relevant platform policies (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store) and advertising network policies (e.g., Google AdMob, AppLovin).

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your email address, when you:

  • Contact us directly for support or inquiries.
  • Opt-in to receive specific communications or newsletters (if such features are introduced).
  • Create an optional user account for personalized features (if such features are introduced).

We do not otherwise collect directly personally identifiable information from you when you use the TS IPTV app, such as your name, phone number, or precise location data.

b. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected):

We may collect non-personal information about your usage of the App and device to improve its performance, provide relevant advertising, and enhance user experience. This may include:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), IP address, mobile network information, screen resolution, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as channels watched, features accessed, duration of use, crash reports, and interaction with advertisements. This data is primarily collected in an aggregated and anonymized form where possible, **but may also be analyzed in more detail to personalize in-app content and experiences.**
  • Log Data: Information that your device sends whenever you visit our App, suchs as access times and dates, and other statistics.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, maintain, and improve the App's functionality, content, and user experience.
  • To understand and analyze how you use the App and to develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • To monitor and analyze trends and usage patterns, and to detect, prevent, and address technical issues.
  • To provide personalized content and recommendations within the App, **including tailoring content and features based on your past interactions within the app and any preferences you explicitly set.**
  • To display relevant advertisements, as described in Section 3 (Third-Party Service Providers).
  • To communicate with you, **including responding to your inquiries and, if you have opted in, sending you updates or newsletters related to the App.**
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party services in the App for analytics and advertising. These providers may collect, use, and share your information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand their data practices.

a. Advertising (Google AdMob, AppLovin, etc.):

We use advertising services like Google AdMob and AppLovin to serve advertisements within our App. These providers may collect and use your device identifier (e.g., Advertising ID) and other information about your interests and app usage to provide personalized ads. This helps us to offer the App for free.

  • Google AdMob: For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and how Google uses information from apps or sites that use their services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • AppLovin: For more information on AppLovin's privacy practices, please visit their Privacy Policy: https://www.applovin.com/privacy/.
  • You can typically reset your device's advertising ID in your device settings, which may limit the collection of personalized data. You may also be able to opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings (e.g., Android: Google Settings > Ads; iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising).

b. Analytics (Firebase, Google Analytics):

We use Firebase and Google Analytics for aggregated analytics regarding app usage. These tools help us understand how users interact with our App, identify trends, and improve features. Firebase may collect data like crash reporting, performance monitoring, and analytics events. This data is generally anonymized and aggregated, **but may also be used to understand user behavior for content and experience personalization.**

  • Google Firebase: For more information on Firebase's data processing, please visit Google's Firebase Privacy and Security page: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For more information on Google Analytics, please visit Google's Privacy Policy: https://legal.applovin.com.

c. Third-Party IPTV Sources:

The TS IPTV app allows you to import M3U and Xtream Codes to access content from third-party sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party providers whose content you access through the App.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

  • You can control personalized ads via your device settings (e.g., reset your advertising ID or opt-out of ad personalization).
  • **If we collect your email address, you may have the right to access, update, or request its deletion by contacting us.**
