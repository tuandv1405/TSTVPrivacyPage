Privacy Policy for TS IPTV

This Privacy Policy describes how TS IPTV ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application TS IPTV (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you when you use the TS IPTV app. We do not require you to register an account or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Non-Personal Information:

We may collect non-personal information about your usage of the App to improve its performance and user experience. This may include:

  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device, suchs as the device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, such as the channels watched, features accessed, duration of use, and crash reports. This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Information

The non-personal information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App's functionality.
  • To improve the App's performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To understand how users interact with the App and identify areas for improvement.
  • To monitor and analyze trends and usage patterns.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any other commercial purposes.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal or non-personal information with third parties. As we do not collect personal information, there is no personal information to share.

Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be shared with our analytics providers to help us analyze app usage. This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your information. While we do not collect sensitive personal data, we implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard the non-personal information we collect from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Some content providers might use cookies or other tracking technologies. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that we do not own or control.

Our App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
